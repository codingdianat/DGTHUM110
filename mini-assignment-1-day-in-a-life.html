Mini Assignment 1: Day in a life

Diana Tran

Attached below is my day in a life assignment!

The theme of my video is to show my friend’s daily struggle with leaving their comfortable bed. Similar to other day in a life videos, my friend is shown going through the motions of the day: brushing teeth, going to class, studying etc. There is a bit of a twist because their desire to nap is personified as a Snorlax stuffy who is insistent on getting my friend to return to bed. I was inspired by how well the documentary, The Social Dilemna, utilizes the seven elements of effective storytelling to make facts more entertaining. I was specifically inspired by how they personified the social media apps. Since my Publii website is very early in development, I do not believe that my website is ready for my final project. I envision that this website will hold my final project and have a seamless user experience. I believe that my website will be interactive and require users to scroll through my page. One change that I would like is to incorporate more features that Publii does not have. For example, I  would like to include a slow reveal or some animations. This would require me to incorporate HTML/CSS, which I do not have a ton of experience in. 

Overall, my experience creating the short film was very entertaining and insightful. I believe that I was able to effectively capture my main idea in a creative way. I was very pleased with how the incorporation of a stuffed animal highlighted the main point of my film. One major surprise throughout the filming process was how long it took to capture all the shots. I was under the false impression that the entire filming process for this two minute video would take under an hour; it ended up taking almost two hours! I realized that in order to tell the story that I wanted, I had to be very articulate about the scenes that I am capturing. I learned that the way that a scene is shot influences the mood that it creates. In addition to how a scene is shot, the music that is played behind a video also influences the mood. I found that I was scrolling through all 200 free imovie sound samples looking for the perfect sound. I initially picked a song that did not match the vibe that I was going for. I did not recognize the issue at first and was confused why my video did not portray what I wanted. After incorporating the right soundtrack, text and videos, I was able to create a short film that I was proud of.
